About Subway Touchpoint Spots

This website is a personal wayfinding project that explores two iconic areas of Tokyo — the underground maze of Shinjuku and the bustling streets of Shibuya — offering my own perspective and suggestions for navigating these layered spaces.

Shinjuku Station


Shinjuku is also notoriously complex and confusing to navigate.
                  Especially, the station is a sub-center of Tokyo, serving an average of 3 million
passengers per day across 16 train and subway lines. Even with Google Maps, it’s easy to get
lost—especially underground, where pinpointing your exact location can be tricky. But don’t
worry! if you keep things simple, you can avoid wasting time and get out quickly.
