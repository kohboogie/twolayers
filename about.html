Welcome to Shinjuku!


Shinjuku is a buzzing district of Tokyo, home of the Shinjuku station.
                  When you’re planning a trip to Japan, visiting Shinjuku will always be an option.
A station bursting to the seams, armies of office workers, school kids and international
travelers squeeze through Shinjuku Station's ticket gates on a daily basis—out into a
sprawling area of towering skyscrapers, wide shopping streets and countless cafes, bars and restaurants.
